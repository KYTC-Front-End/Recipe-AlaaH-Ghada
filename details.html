Chicken Noodels

30 Minutes 4.5

Ingredients

1 packet makarona

2 tomatoes

1/2 chicken pieses

2 Broccoli vegetable

Instructions

Prepare the ingredients that includ macaron, vegetables,chicken,oil,spaice,salt

Boil the pasta in a hot water with a little salt and oil.

Cut vegetables, tomatoes and broccoli into small cubes and cook over low heat.

Cook the chicken pieces on low heat, then add cooked vegetables to the chicken pieces.

Mix boiled pasta with chicken and vegetables mixture finally served it with a dish.
